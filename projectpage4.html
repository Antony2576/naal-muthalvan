Sweet and Traditional Indian Treats

Gulab Jamun
  • Ingredients:
    • Milk powder
    • All-purpose flour
    • Baking soda
    • Ghee
    • Milk (to knead)
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom
    • Rose water
  • Steps:
    1. Make soft dough using milk powder, flour, baking soda, and milk.
    2. Roll into balls and deep fry on low flame.
    3. Make sugar syrup with cardamom and rose water.
    4. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for a few hours.
Rasgulla
  • Ingredients:
    • Full-fat milk
    • Lemon juice (to curdle milk)
    • Sugar
    • Water
    • Cardamom
  • Steps:
    1. Boil milk and curdle it to get chenna (paneer).
    2. Drain and knead the chenna until smooth.
    3. Make small balls and cook in boiling sugar syrup.
    4. Let them rest and absorb syrup fully.
Kheer
  • Ingredients:
    • Full-fat milk
    • Basmati rice
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom
    • Saffron (optional)
    • Nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios)
  • Steps:
    1. Wash and soak rice for 15 minutes.
    2. Boil milk and add rice, stir occasionally.
    3. Cook until rice is soft and milk is thickened.
    4. Add sugar, cardamom, and nuts. Simmer and serve chilled or warm.
Jalebi
  • Ingredients:
    • All-purpose flour
    • Yogurt
    • Rice flour (optional)
    • Sugar
    • Saffron and cardamom
    • Ghee or oil (for frying)
  • Steps:
    1. Make batter and let it ferment for a few hours.
    2. Prepare sugar syrup with saffron and cardamom.
    3. Pipe batter in spiral shapes into hot ghee/oil.
    4. Fry until golden and soak in syrup.
Besan Ladoo
  • Ingredients:
    • Gram flour (besan)
    • Ghee
    • Powdered sugar
    • Cardamom powder
    • Chopped nuts (optional)
  • Steps:
    1. Dry roast besan in ghee until golden and aromatic.
    2. Cool slightly and mix in powdered sugar and cardamom.
    3. Shape into small balls while still warm.
Rasmalai
  • Ingredients:
    • Chenna (paneer)
    • Full-fat milk
    • Sugar
    • Saffron
    • Cardamom
    • Nuts
  • Steps:
    1. Prepare soft rasgullas from chenna.
    2. Boil milk until thickened, add saffron & cardamom.
    3. Soak rasgullas in sweetened milk.
Sandesh
  • Ingredients:
    • Chenna (paneer)
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom
    • Saffron (optional)
  • Steps:
    1. Knead chenna until smooth.
    2. Cook lightly with sugar.
    3. Shape into patties or decorative molds.
Modak
  • Ingredients:
    • Rice flour
    • Fresh grated coconut
    • Jaggery
    • Cardamom
  • Steps:
    1. Make a soft rice flour dough.
    2. Prepare coconut-jaggery filling with cardamom.
    3. Shape modaks with filling inside.
    4. Steam until cooked.
Mysore Pak
  • Ingredients:
    • Gram flour
    • Ghee
    • Sugar
  • Steps:
    1. Roast gram flour in ghee.
    2. Prepare sugar syrup and mix with roasted flour.
    3. Pour mixture into a greased tray and set.
    4. Cut into squares when cool.
Soan Papdi
  • Ingredients:
    • Gram flour
    • All-purpose flour
    • Ghee
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom
    • Nuts
  • Steps:
    1. Roast flour in ghee.
    2. Prepare sugar syrup and mix well.
    3. Stretch mixture into flaky layers.
    4. Cut into cubes and garnish with nuts.
Malpua
  • Ingredients:
    • All-purpose flour
    • Milk
    • Fennel seeds
    • Sugar
    • Ghee (for frying)
  • Steps:
    1. Prepare a smooth batter with flour, milk, and fennel.
    2. Fry small pancakes in ghee.
    3. Dip in sugar syrup before serving.
Shrikhand
  • Ingredients:
    • Hung curd (strained yogurt)
    • Sugar
    • Saffron
    • Cardamom powder
  • Steps:
    1. Whisk hung curd until smooth.
    2. Add sugar and mix well.
    3. Flavor with saffron and cardamom.
    4. Chill before serving.
