egg curry
  • Ingredients:
    • Chicken or mutton
    • Basmati rice
    • Yogurt, fried onions, saffron
    • Whole spices: bay leaf, cardamom, cloves
  • Steps:
    1. Marinate meat in yogurt and spices.
    2. Parboil rice with whole spices.
    3. Layer rice and meat, add fried onions & saffron milk.
    4. Cook on dum (sealed pot) for 30–40 minutes.
grilled tandoori chicken
  • Ingredients:
    • Flattened rice (poha)
    • Onion, mustard seeds, curry leaves
    • Green chilies, turmeric, peanuts
    • Lemon juice, coriander
  • Steps:
    1. Rinse poha and set aside.
    2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
    3. Sauté onions, then add turmeric and poha.
    4. Mix well, top with peanuts and lemon juice.
crab masala
  • Ingredients:
    • Besan (gram flour)
    • Yogurt, ginger-green chili paste
    • Fruit salt (Eno), mustard seeds
    • Curry leaves, sugar, lemon
  • Steps:
    1. Make a smooth batter with besan and yogurt.
    2. Add Eno just before steaming.
    3. Steam for 15–20 mins, cool, and cut into pieces.
    4. Pour tempered mustard-curry leaf mix on top.
